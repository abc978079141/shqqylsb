    QIQU FUN COMPANY PROFILE

    GUANGZHOU QIQU FUN Co., Ltd. is located in Chinese international metropolis, International Trade Center------Guangzhou, is a comprehensive enterprise specializing in the production of Children's amusement, is a set design, development, production, sales as one of professional enterprises of indoor and outdoor children's amusement.

    QIQU FUN has a large number of outstanding designers, engineers, sales and technical management personnel, Using the advanced CAD/CAM design system, according to the site plan provided by the customer. In view of the different situation and special requirements of the customers, the most reasonable scheme is designed.

     

    QIQU FUN has a large modern standard workshop and office building, all aspects and features of the product showroom. Over the years, QIQU FUN always adhere to market-oriented, customer demand as its mission, and gradually formed a large toy, combination slide, Hand boat equipment, children's toys, children's furniture products system, It mainly provides all kinds of amusement equipment for kindergartens, schools, parks, street community, residential district, tourist attractions, resort a large supermarket, and a variety of theme parks and other public places.

    Since its inception in 1995, QIQU FUN adhere to the "quality first, reputation first, service first" principle, all products are using environmentally friendly materials, products through the national sporting goods testing center, the quality is insured by the People's Insurance Company of China, and implement the ISO9001 international management system and quality assurance system.

    QIQU FUN has strong strength, pay attention to reputation and keep the promise, the products are widely sold all over the world, and are exported to the Middle East, Southeast Asia, Africa, Europe, America and Russia and other countries and regions, they are well received by customers.
