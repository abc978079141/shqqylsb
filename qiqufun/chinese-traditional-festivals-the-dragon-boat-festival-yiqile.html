    Chinese traditional festivals——The Dragon Boat Festival-QIQU FUN
    2018-06-18

    In June 18, 2018, the Dragon Boat Festival, which has been passed on for thousands of years in China.We held an event on this day to watch the strong atmosphere of the dragon boat race experience festival, according to the traditional,we eat glutinous rice dumplings made of glutinous rice, boiled eggs and boiled duck eggs, to carry out the festival cleaning, hang the wormwood leaves and sticks, hang the calamus, and spray the male yellow water.

    I hope everyone can learn more about Chinese Culture, and interested friends can come to China to experience it!

    I wish you all good health and happy Dragon Boat Festival!
