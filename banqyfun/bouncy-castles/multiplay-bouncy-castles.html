Multiplay Bouncy castles

Multiplay bouncy castles for sale

Multiplay bouncy castles are very popular with smaller kids ages 4 – 10. With it’s inflatable obstacles, punching bags, climbing wall and slide, the multiplay bouncers are among our most sought after models. Our range of multiplays have been our best selling bouncy castles for many years. Customers from all over the world value this particular range of product. We do sell our multiplay bouncy castles often to hiring companies, but schools, councils, churches and sporting clubs are big fans of the multiplay concept as well.

Multiplay castles can be purchase with or without a roof! We first introduced this type of bouncy castle 11 years ago and we have been constantly developing and improving it ever since. This has lead to our current range of over 16 different multiplay bouncy castles with  popular themes such as Crocodile, Shark, Elephant, Dolphin and Pirate.

Manufacturer of multiplay bouncy castles

Besides the multiplays from our range, we also manufacture bespoke bouncy castles with the same features. We’ve made thousands of bespoke bouncy castles with integrated slides, tunnels and inflatable objects. Custom artwork and advertising can be added as well. Contact us today to make your idea’s a reality!
