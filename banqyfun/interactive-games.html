Interactive games

Looking to expand the business with the most attractive, most out of this world, or the most innovative inflatable games? Look no further! JB-inflatables has Europe’s largest stock including: inflatable slides, air mountains, obstacle courses and other inflatable games. With our Interactive games you will ‘wow’ your clients and provide them with many hours of fun and entertainment! Furthermore, these inflatables are tested commercial grade inflatables and are sure to bring your company some decent revenue.

Our Interactive games include using skills such as running, swimming, jumping, hand-eye coordination and much more!

Interactive games
