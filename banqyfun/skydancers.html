Skydancers

JB-Inflatables manufactures skydancers in the Netherlands. There we have 160m² sewing room where we can make the skydancers in any size, color or shape. Furthermore, we are specialized in custom made skytubes with logo’s, text or other forms of advertising. An ever moving giant inflatable such as a skydancer is the perfect way to draw attention to your brand or business.

Skydancers: Raising Awareness

Skydancers are made to order. Notably, you can use them to raise awareness for a cause, campaign, a race or just to add a little pizazz to your grand opening. Uniquely, we also manufacture a lot of promotional inflatables for sport clubs and –events such as: the Olympics, FIFA world cup, the Volvo Ocean race and the Amstel Gold race. Just contact us to discuss your wishes!

Skilled design team

Now you can also make your own designs, with our amazing Design Simulator!

Within the JB-Inflatables team we have our skilled design team! These amazing and talented people are more than happy to design your perfect promotional inflatable.  You choose all colours, dimentions, designs, specifications and so on! All you have to do is contact us today, it’s absolutely free!
